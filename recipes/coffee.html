Coffee with milk recipe

Description

In this tutorial we will learn how to make Coffee with milk, A very good morning starter for the hectic life of the 21st century

Igredients

  • Coffee
  • Milk
  • Sugar(Optional)

Instructions

  1. Put milk in a pan and make it boil at high heat
  2. Once the milk almost spills out of the pan, turn off the heat
  3. Pour your hot milk in a cup
  4. Add your favorite ground of instant coffee in da milk
  5. Add sugar if you wish
  6. Congratulation, you have boosted your morning mood !
