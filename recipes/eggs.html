Boiled eggs recipe

  1. Put water in a pan and make it boil at high heat
  2. Once the water starts boiling put your eggs in it
  3. Let the eggs boil at high heat for 12minuts
  4. Once the 12minutes has passed, turn off the heat and leave your eggs in the hot water for another 10minutes
  5. Your Boiled Eggs are ready, Enjoy !
